Card Shuffling Game Instructions

Objective:

Guess the number you select after a series of card shuffles.

Instructions:

  1. Choose a Number: Select a number between 1 and 21.
  2. Choose a Card: There are three cards labeled A, B, and C. Each card contains 7 numbers. Click on a card containing the number you thought about.
  3. Continue Shuffling: Click the "Continue" button. The cards will be shuffled, hiding the chosen number.
  4. Guess the Card: After shuffling, select the card which contains your chosen number. Click on that card and Continue.
  5. Final Guess: Repeat the process one more time. Click "Continue," guess the card, and see if you were correct.
  6. See the Result: The game will reveal the chosen number. If you guessed correctly, congratulations! If not, you can try again.

Tips:

  • Pay attention to the card movements during shuffling.
  • Think strategically to track your chosen number across the shuffles.
  • Have fun and keep practicing to improve your guessing skills!
